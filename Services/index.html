Health and Wellness

Health Checks: What are they and why does your pet need one?

Some owners may question why it is necessary to bring your pets for regular check-ups and the main reason for this is that pets cannot tell us when something is wrong! As animals age, the tendency to develop certain diseases becomes more likely. Keeping a regular check on their health and welfare with your vet can help prevent your animals from being in pain. At our veterinary practice, we offer services such as regular health checks, surgery and dental check-ups. With our busy lives, these issues are often overlooked and we are here to help ensure your pet gets the quality treatment they deserve.

Your health check will entail your vet examining the history of your pet, whether it be changes in their behaviour or specific health questions. They will commence and examination and then proceed to address the concern in question.

At our clinic, we recommend bringing your pet for a check-up at least once a year for a full body examination. We provide an in-depth assessment of your pet's overall physical health. These inspections include:
  • Eye and Ear Examination
  • Heart and Lung Analysis
  • Temperature Checks
  • Abdominal Readings
  • Dental Examination
  • Musculoskeletal Evaluatoin
  • Behavioural Counseling
  • Weight and Nutrition Counseling
For your convenience we operate an appointment based system. If you're in need of an emergency appointment outside of work hours, please contact 555-337255.

Our practice strive to offer the most advanced surgical procedures using modern technology. All animals are carefully screened for safety with previous health checks and blood tests. Our surgeries include:
  • Spay Neutered
  • Wound Care
  • Lump Biopsy and Removals
  • Ophthalmic Procedures
We always ensure to deliver the most up to date pain relief so your pet will enjoy the freedom from pain.

Like humans, pets can also develop gum diseases. We will develop a dental plan focusing on removing tartar build up with regular brushing and trimming if necessary (in the case of rabbits). A change in diet may also be implemented in order to prevent various issues such as gingivitis (dogs) and eosinophilic granulomas (cats).

Vaccinations


What diseases are my pet's protected from vaccinations?


Your cat is protected against...

Feline Panleukopenia Virus

Feline Herpesvirus

Feline Calicivirus

Feline Leukaemia Virus

Chlamydophila Felis

Bordetella Bronchiseptica


Your dog is protected against...

Parovirus

Distemper

Parainfluenza

Leptospirosis

Hepatitis Contagiosa Canis

Rabies


Vaccinations are a lifesaver for pets. Give them the warmth of love with proper medical care. At our clinic we offer affordable cat and dog vaccinations to ensure your pet is protected from deadly infections. Contact us over phone or email for discount vaccination bundles.

Pet Care Services


Dog Walking

Our experienced dog walkers will provide your dog with the exercise, attention and care they need while you work. Your dog can be walked alone or with a group. Personalised programs are available.

Price Range: €10-€50

Home Visits

Home visits typically involve calling to your house once or twice daily to take care of your pet(s). Ideal for pets that are extremely attached to their home surroundings.

Price Range: €20-€100

Pet Taxi

A pet taxi is a transportation service for your pet. Our service allows your pet(s) to be driven unaccompanied to and from the location of your choice by our fully accredited pet taxi drivers.

Price Range: Varies by Location

Grooming

Our typical professional grooming session consists of your pet being brushed, bathed and dried, as well as trimmed or clipped with high quaality brushes and clipper blades.

Price Range: €25-€80

24 Hour Care

Pets can become ill when we least expect it. We provide an Emergency Vet service 24/7 to assist you in your pet's time of need. Your pet's safety and welfare is our number one priority.

Price Range: On Call Fee €30
